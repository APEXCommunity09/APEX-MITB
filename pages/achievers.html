Our Achievements

Congratulations: APEX-W Community Member's Research Paper Accepted at ICMLT Conference!
We're delighted to announce that the research paper 'Mobile Price Prediction Using Machine Learning,' authored by APEX-W Community members Disha Panwar and Shadeeksha Shree, has been accepted at the prestigious ICMLT Conference by ACM.
APEX Community Member's Research Paper Accepted at ICMLT Conference!
We are thrilled to share the wonderful news that our APEX Community members, Shivansh Gautam and Shaurya Shandilya, have had their research paper titled "Car Price Prediction Using Machine Learning" accepted at the esteemed International Conference on Machine Learning Technologies (ICMLT), hosted by the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM). This achievement is a testament to their hard work and dedication.
APEX-W Success: Disha Panwar & Gauravi Singh Shine
We're excited to announce the stellar success of Disha Panwar and Gauravi Singh from APEX-W. Both secured coveted summer internships at Hero Housing Finance and Zenerative Minds (IIIT Hyderabad), respectively. Their achievement, in their first college year, is a testament to their dedication and active participation in our workshops. Congratulations to Disha and Gauravi on their remarkable accomplishment!
APEX Success: Shivansh Gautam & Ambikesh Jha Shine
We're thrilled to announce the success of our APEX community members, Shivansh Gautam and Ambikesh Jha, who have secured summer internships at esteemed companies like Dr. Reddy's Laboratories. Congratulations to both for achieving this milestone in their first and second years of college. Their dedication, demonstrated through regular attendance at community workshops and adherence to instructions, has made this possible. Kudos to Shivansh and Ambikesh for their hard work and consistency!
